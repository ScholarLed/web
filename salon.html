SALON

The ScholarLed Salon

The ScholarLed Salon series is an every-other-month gathering of scholar-led presses, devoted to informal discussion and knowledge-sharing. The Salon workshops—offered in the spirit of "brown bag" gatherings—feature a rotating cast of topics of interest to the community. The sessions are open to anyone interested in scholar-led publishing. Occasional sessions will be cross-organized with the Open Access Books Network.
